My favourite hobby

Reading books

I love to read books

My name is Kevin Jacob. Reading fictional books is my favourite hobby beacuse it always gives me something to do and enjoy. You can read the same books again and again and sometimes they are so exiting that you do not feel bored.

Rick Riordan is my favourite author. He is 56 years old. He has written Percy Jackson, Heroes of Olympus, Trials of Apollo, Magnus Chase, Kane Chronicles and Demigods And Magicians. My favourite series of them all is Magnus Chase. All Rick Riordan's books are very exciting.

I know very well that reading is an excellent hobby so I am very happy to have picked it up. It is an excellent source of enjoyment for me.
